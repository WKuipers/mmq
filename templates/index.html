Version 0.1.0 - First version. Hype!

This release adds support for customizing the randomized playback. Just press the favorite button to add songs to your favorite playlist. Once you have favorites mmq will only randomly add favorite songs.

You can view your favorites playlist in the 'playlist' section found in the header. You're not yet able to remove favorites.
